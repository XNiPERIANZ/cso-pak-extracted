The boss of an armed terrorist group, who is now on death row, will be used as a specimen for an experiment conducted by a special laboratory.
Counter-Terrorist Elite Forces have been given the mission of transporting the prisoner to a different location.
The Terrorists have received intelligence about the transportation method and are  attempting to block the path and rescue their boss.

- Counter-Terrorists: Destroy any obstacles along the route and safely transport the prisoner to the target location.
- Terrorists: Disable all the restraining devices on the vehicle transporting the boss.
