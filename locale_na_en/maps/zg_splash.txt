Old Dam
The arrival of Giant Zombies forced workers to abandon their posts. As a result, the dam's facility has been left unmonitored and water has begun to flood the area.

Now, this old dam protects the only humans left to fight against Zombie Giants...

Mission:
- To earn points, Humans must kill zombies and zombies must kill humans.
- The first team to reach the score goal wins.
