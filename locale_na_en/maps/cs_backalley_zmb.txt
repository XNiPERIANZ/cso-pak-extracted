Back Alley - Zombie

Map Created By: TYR (tyr@barking-dog.com)

During the battle between Terrorists and Counter-Terrorists, unidentified beings have been discovered and they appear to be zombies that escaped from the Rex Laboratory. The humans must now work together in order to survive.

- Humans: Survive the Zombie attacks for the remaining duration or kill all the Zombies.
- Zombies: Those who have turned into Zombies must kill the Humans and turn them into Zombies. Zombies have higher HP and faster speeds than Humans.
