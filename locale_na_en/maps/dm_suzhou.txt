Suzhou Annihilation - Annihilation

Planning/Production: NEXON
Texture/Modeling/Revision: NEXON

The Chinese government has received intelligence indicating that Terrorists have been smuggling drugs in a town called Suzhou, located outside of the city. In order to stop the Terrorists, the Counter-Terrorists attacked the town only to discover that Terrorists had been waiting for them. Terrorist scouts have also verified the movement of the Counter-Terrorists and are preparing for an all-out attack.

- Kill all enemy team members on the map.
- When one team is annihilated, the remaining team wins.
