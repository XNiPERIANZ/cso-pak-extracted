Tunnel - Deathmatch

Planning/Production: NEXON
Texture/Revision: NEXON

The Terrorists are planning to blow up a tunnel that a train passes through in order to steal the cash and weapons it is transporting.

- Counter-Terrorists: Attack the Terrorists to prevent them from achieving their goal.
- Terrorists: Attack the Counter-Terrorists so that the Terrorist mission goes according to plan.

Note: Each team has two reinforcement locations on this map.
