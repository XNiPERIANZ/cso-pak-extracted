Rhyzome (RHYZOME) - Zombie Escape
Planned, designed, and developed by: Nexon
Graphics: Nexon

After the Zombie Virus outbreak,
the surviving groups of civilians, soldiers, and police
escaped out towards the outskirts of the city and created their own shelter to stay for the time being.

However, they could not evade the zombie virus forever....

Objective
Humans: Escape to the helipad on the outer edge of the cliff within the time limit. You must remain human.
Zombies: Infect all remaining humans or stop them from reaching the helipad within the time limit.
