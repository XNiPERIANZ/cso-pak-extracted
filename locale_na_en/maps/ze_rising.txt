RISING - Zombie Escape
Planned, designed and developed by: Nexon
Graphics: Nexon

To all survivors, 
There is a rescue heli nearby. 
Please rush to the helipad and signal for rescue with beacon lights and escape.

Objective
Humans: Escape to the helipad within the time limit. You must remain human.
Zombies: Infect all remaining humans or stop them from reaching the helipad within the time limit.
