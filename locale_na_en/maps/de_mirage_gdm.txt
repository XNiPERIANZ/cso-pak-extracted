Mirage (Mirage) - Gun Deathmatch
Developed and produced by: Nexon
Texture/Modeling/Edited by: Nexon

A large-scale Counter-Terrorist mission aims to remove the last vestiges of Terrorist activity in the area.
However, the Terrorists have resupplied with powerful weapons and intend to fight to the last.

Objective
- You get a new weapon when you reach a certain number of kills against enemies with that weapon.
- To win, eliminate the enemy team with the final grenade given during the final moments of the game.
