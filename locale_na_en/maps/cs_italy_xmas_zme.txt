Italy - X-Mas - Zombie War

Map created by: Nexon
Texture/graphics created by: Nexon

During the battle between Terrorists and Counter-Terrorists, strange, undead creatures have emerged from the Rex Laboratory.
The humans have no choice but to work together to survive.

Objective
Humans: Survive the zombie attacks until time runs out, or kill all the zombies.
Zombies: Kill all the humans. Zombies have more HP and are faster than humans.
