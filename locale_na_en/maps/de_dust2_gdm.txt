Dust 2 - Gun Deathmatch

Map Created By: DaveJ (http://www.johnsto.co.uk/)
Texture Created By: MacMan (MacManInfi@aol.com)

Objective
- The team that kills an enemy team member with a final grenade wins.
- Raise the team score by killing all enemy team members on the map.
- Reach the designated kill count within the time limit to win.
