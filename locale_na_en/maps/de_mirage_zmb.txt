Mirage (Mirage) - Zombie
Map created by: Nexon
Texture created by: Nexon

The undead hordes have finally made their way to the arid deserts of the world. Those humans who have taken refuge there must once again fight for survival.


Humans: Survive the zombie attacks until time runs out or kill all the zombies.
Zombies: Once you become a zombie, try to kill the remaining humans and turn them into zombies. Zombies have higher HP and greater speed than humans.
