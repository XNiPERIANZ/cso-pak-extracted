Downed - Hostage Rescue

A civilian airplane was attacked by a Terrorist RPG and has crashed into the jungle. The Terrorists have taken civilians hostage and are demanding a large amount of money.

- Counter-Terrorists: Rescue the hostages. Neutralize the Terrorists without killing the hostages.
- Terrorists: Use all means necessary to prevent the Counter-Terrorist rescue attempt.

Note: There are 4 hostages in this mission.
