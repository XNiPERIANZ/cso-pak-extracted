Bunny Yard – Gun Deathmatch
Direct / Production: Nexon
Texture/Modelling/Edit: Nexon

After ending the previous operation in success, the government plans additional operations to eliminate the remaining terrorist force. 
The terrorist violently resists this, and goes into a stand-off.

Objective
- The team emerges victorious if a player manages to kill the enemy player with the Final Grenade.
- In order to increase the team score, the players must eliminate enemy players.
- The team emerges victorious if the team reaches the goal score first.
