Special Forces those who were dispatched to investigate the mysterious Infection could do nothing but retreating from transfectants. They kept retreating and finally they ended up at a small base in the shore.

They hid themselves and what they could do was waiting for the rescue team. However, there was one person who was infected and started to turn on of them. 

Others didn't know what would soon come.

- Humans: Prepare for the Zombie attacks and secure the safety zone and survive until the dawn.
- Zombies: Infect everyone.
