Forbidden - Zombie

Planning/Production: NEXON
Texture/Revision: NEXON

Near the illegal chemical laboratory... was a chemical treatment facility that treated experiment waste materials. However, its operation had ceased a long time ago. It has now become a sanctuary for Zombies who've grown stronger thanks to the various chemical waste.

- Humans: Kill all the Zombies or survive until the time duration has passed.
- Zombies: Those who have turned into Zombies must kill the Humans and turn them into Zombies. Zombies have higher HP and faster speeds than Humans.
